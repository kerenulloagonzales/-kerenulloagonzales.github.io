Why I Love This Topic

I have always been drawn to the deep questions of existence. Why are we here? Is there a God? How do we know what is true? These questions have led me to consume debates, lectures, and books from thinkers across the spectrum — from Christian apologists to atheist philosophers. I believe engaging with these questions honestly and openly makes us better thinkers and better people.
